the brand

TadiBrothers is one of the leading brands for high-end backup camera systems for small and large vehicles.

the goal

TadiBrothers had a high revenue share from email when they reached out. They were looking to take their emails to the next level while improving their deliverability.

the problem

Flows were set up in a way that people were constantly getting emails.

We changed the setup to reduce the email load. The idea was that, if people were getting fewer emails, they were more likely to open the ones they got. This would lead to better engagement and, in turn, better deliverability.
